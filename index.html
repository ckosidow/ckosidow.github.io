Agility is Key

I'm a lover of agile development and extreme programming. I'm constantly looking for the fastest and most effective way to both deliver code and perform cohesively with my team. It's not always the most fun when functioning code has to change, but it can be both enjoyable and expedient when done correctly.

group

Lover of Teams

I tend to agree with Robert C. Martin's statement in Agile Software Development Principles Patterns and Practices that "Working well with others, communicating and interacting, is more important than raw programming talent." Poor teammwork slows the development process; so I advocate for strong communication and teamwork at every turn.

The Nitty Gritty

Perhaps my favorite thing of all is learning all the tools available to me in development. You can never spend enough time learning what capabilities make a language powerful, but I do my best to practice and learn more about the languages and tools I use as well as those I don't.

Outside of Work

When I'm not working I like to read books about programming languages, patterns, processes and design. I also enjoy many other books. Some of which include Great Expectations, A Song of Ice and Fire and How to Win Friends and Influence People. I also enjoy exercising, meditating and practicing foreign languages daily. These are some of the things that keep me sane, and this is my life distilled into one short paragraph.
